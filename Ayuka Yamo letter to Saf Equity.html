They wait 6-8 hours at overcrowded hospitals. They travel hundreds of kilometers. They spend money they don't have. And often, they still don't get the right answers.

It's not just an app or an AI system. It's 15 years of medical knowledge, thousands of patient cases, proven treatment protocols, and comprehensive diagnostic frameworks - all packaged into a platform that a farmer in Turkana can access for KSh 2,500. No travel. No waiting. No guesswork.
